# Land And Deploy

Source: GStack by Garry Tan, MIT licensed, https://github.com/garrytan/gstack.

Prepare merge/deploy readiness: reviews, tests, release notes, rollback,
monitoring, and approval. Do not merge or deploy without explicit Paperclip
approval.
