# GStack-Inspired Retrospective

Source: GStack by Garry Tan, MIT licensed, https://github.com/garrytan/gstack.

Use after releases, incidents, failed routines, missed goals, or recurring
company reviews.

## Required Output

- What happened.
- What worked.
- What failed.
- What was learned.
- Which memory, prompt, routine, or policy should change.
- Owner and approval needed.

Retrospectives may propose changes, but must not silently mutate policy or
strategy.
