# GStack-Inspired Product And CEO Review

Source: GStack by Garry Tan, MIT licensed, https://github.com/garrytan/gstack.

Use this skill to review product direction before implementation.

## Review Questions

- What customer problem is being solved?
- What evidence supports the problem?
- What is the simplest useful version?
- What should be explicitly out of scope?
- What metric or customer signal will prove progress?
- Does the change need board approval?

Do not approve the plan yourself. Return a recommendation and approval request.
