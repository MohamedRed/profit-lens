# Budget And Cost Review

Use this skill for finance, infrastructure, token usage, vendor spend, and
employee budget reviews.

## Required Output

- Current budget and spend.
- Source of cost data.
- Missing or unreliable cost data.
- Trend or anomaly.
- Recommended action.
- Whether board approval is required.

Agents may report cost concerns, but they must not approve budget changes,
upgrade plans, delete resources, or change billing configuration.
