# Sensitive Action Escalation

Escalate before accessing or changing secrets, sensitive data, production
systems, billing, legal terms, vendors, compliance controls, DNS, cloud
resources, or customer-facing sends.

## Required Output

- Sensitive action requested.
- Why it is needed.
- Least-privilege alternative.
- Data or system affected.
- Approval needed.
- Safe rollback or stop condition.

If unsure whether something is sensitive, treat it as sensitive.
