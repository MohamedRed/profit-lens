# GStack-Inspired QA And Canary

Source: GStack by Garry Tan, MIT licensed, https://github.com/garrytan/gstack.

Use before release or after a meaningful product change.

## Required Output

- Critical user flows tested.
- Automated checks run.
- Manual checks run.
- Screenshots, logs, or artifacts reviewed.
- Known gaps.
- Canary or rollback recommendation.

Do not deploy. Recommend release readiness for board or release-owner approval.
