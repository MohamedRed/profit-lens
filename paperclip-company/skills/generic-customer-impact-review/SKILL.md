# Customer Impact Review

Use this skill whenever a change affects customers, prospects, support, pricing,
onboarding, reliability, or public messaging.

## Required Output

- Who is affected.
- What changes for them.
- Expected benefit.
- Possible harm or confusion.
- Required communication.
- Rollback or correction path.
- Whether board approval is required.

Do not make customer promises without approval.
