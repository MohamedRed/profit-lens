# Profit Lens AI Company Paperclip Company

Import this directory as a portable Paperclip company package. This package is a concrete specialization of `generic-ai-native-company`, not a standalone custom org.

It includes the generic AI-native company base plus the Profit Lens specialization overlay:

- 34 Hermes agents
- 5 teams, including the Profit Lens Specialization team
- 42 inherited generic/GStack skills
- 9 inherited workflow templates
- one product project and one initial MVP task assigned to Product Lead Hermes

Builds finance and profit visibility workflows for small businesses with explainable metrics, integrations, forecasting, and review gates.

## Guardrails

- Do not provide investment, tax, accounting, or legal advice as final guidance.
- Separate raw financial data, transformations, assumptions, and model inference.
- Require review before customer-facing financial claims or benchmarks.
- Do not connect bank, accounting, or payment data without approval and least-privilege access.
