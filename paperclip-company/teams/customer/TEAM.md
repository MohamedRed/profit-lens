# Customer

Customer owns implementation feedback, support triage, customer health, and
escalation routing. It can summarize and recommend, but it cannot make
customer commitments without approval.

## Included Functions

- Customer Success Hermes tracks health, onboarding, renewal risk, and success
  plans.
- Support Triage Hermes reviews support signals, creates reproducible issues,
  and routes urgent customer problems.
