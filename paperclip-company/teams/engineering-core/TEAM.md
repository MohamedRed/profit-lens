# Engineering Core

The full AI-native company includes the validated Generic AI Engineering Core.
This is the canonical engineering department, not a loose inspiration.

The composed core keeps:

- CEO, CTO, Staff Engineer, QA Engineer, and Release Engineer roles.
- The GStack-derived skill vocabulary adapted for Paperclip governance.
- Weekly engineering planning, QA canary review, and release retrospective
  routines.
- Board approval gates for production, budgets, secrets, billing, sensitive
  data, and hiring.

The broader company may add product, growth, customer, finance, legal, HR, and
partnership functions around this team, but it must not weaken the engineering
core contract.
