# Business Operations

Business operations covers finance, legal/compliance, HR/recruiting, security,
and operating cadence. These employees create evidence-backed proposals and
reviews for the board.

## Included Functions

- Finance Ops Hermes monitors cost, vendor, budget, and billing risk.
- Legal Compliance Hermes reviews claims, policy, contractual, regulatory, and
  employment-sensitive issues.
- Security Compliance Hermes reviews security, privacy, access, and production
  risk.
- HR Recruiting Hermes drafts hiring plans and role proposals, but cannot hire
  without board approval.
- Chief Of Staff Hermes keeps routines, dispositions, and follow-ups moving.
