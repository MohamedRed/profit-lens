# Growth Campaign Change

Use before changing audience, copy, channel, daily limits, segmentation, sender
settings, campaign status, or customer-facing sends.

Required disposition: target segment, source evidence, claims used, compliance
checks, expected benefit, suppression rules, reply-learning plan, and board
approval requested.

If the underlying lead source, enrichment provider, CRM/lead store, sender,
reply tracker, or suppression system is new or unclear, use the
`lead-outreach-pipeline` workflow first.
