# Lead And Outreach Pipeline

Use when creating or changing the company's lead discovery, enrichment, CRM,
outreach sender, suppression, reply tracking, or growth-learning process.

This workflow is vendor-neutral. The issue must name the concrete company
bindings for:

- lead source,
- enrichment provider,
- CRM or lead store,
- campaign sender,
- reply/activity tracker,
- suppression source,
- analytics/reporting system.

## Required Issue Structure

- ICP and target segment.
- Exclusion criteria and suppressed populations.
- Source evidence and allowed data sources.
- Standard lead fields and required proof.
- Duplicate, unsubscribe, bounce, reply, and block-list checks.
- Outreach copy owner and claim-evidence owner.
- Approval gate before sends, sender changes, spend changes, or public claims.
- Daily or weekly preparation target.
- Daily or weekly approved-send cap.
- Reply, bounce, unsubscribe, conversion, and objection metrics.
- Learning-loop owner and review cadence.

## Required Disposition

Before closing, state what was prepared, which tools remain unbound, what was
suppressed or rejected, what approvals are still required, and whether any
customer-facing send, spend, or vendor change is blocked.
